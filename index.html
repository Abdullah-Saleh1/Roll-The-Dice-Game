How The Game Works? ...

  • At first you have to roll the dice (you can roll the dice more than once if you want).
  • Each time you roll the dice, the points will be added to the current score (but be careful if you get the number 1, you will lose the current points and switch to the next player).
  • Now that you have collected some points, you must hold these points by pressing the "Hold" button, then the points will be added to the total score.
  • the player who reaches 100 total points or more wins the game.
